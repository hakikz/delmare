A new view of
Bayside luxury.

Exclusive townhouse living by the bay.

Welcome to Del Mare, 6 distinguished townhomes nestled in one of Melbourne’s best beachside suburbs, Bonbeach.
Del Mare is just a short stroll to the pristine stretch of white sand beach on Port Phillip, Bonbeach, 1.6 km of beach that extends from Chelsea southwards to the Patterson River.
Begin your day walking or running along sands before a refreshing dip, or head to the many cafes for a coffee, or a round of golf at Patterson River Golf Club; every day will be perfect living at Del Mare.

Timeless design

With a timeless design that takes its cues from the surrounding suburb, Del Mare sits gently among its neighbours, subtly expressing its luxurious style in high-quality materials and excellent craftsmanship. The elegant townhouses bring together the creative expertise, and design skills of the Joseph Galati Architects with landscaped gardens create properties that offer a luxurious lifestyle in this prestigious inner-bayside suburb.

Education

Excellent schools are easily accessible for bayside residents. Del Mare is in the zone for Patterson River Secondary College which merged in 1992 with the nearby Bonbeach High School staying on the site of Seaford-Carrum. Bonbeach Primary School established in 1958 is unique in the area having a Pre-school on the same location as the school providing 3 and 4-year-old programs through to Year 6.

They are inspired to achieve excellence in every home they develop, and select the most beautiful locations in Melbourne’s premium suburbs. Del Mare Bonbeach has been designed to take advantage of its unique seaside location, large kitchens perfect the home entertainer, large stone benches with a waterfall island, high-quality Bosch appliances, finishes and design.

The Architect

Joseph Galati Architecture is a well respected architectural design practice locally based in Melbourne. Their design philosophy is to create beautiful architecture that evokes excitement, functionality and efficiency with the utmost respect to its natural environment, client brief and community. Del Mare is the latest excellent example of his work, with a contemporary architectural response within its natural bayside environment. With the creation of 6 individual townhomes, orientated to take advantage of the site surrounds, and provide the highest amenity for bayside residential living. Expressive in its form with its clean lines, varied material palette and integrated landscaping, each townhome is designed with spacious living areas filled with natural light, and seamless transition to the interior and exterior spaces.
